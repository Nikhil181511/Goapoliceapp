Goa Police Cyber Patrol ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, stored, and shared.

## Data Collection
- We collect user account information (such as email, username) for authentication.
- We may collect usage data to improve app performance and security.
- We do not collect sensitive personal information without your consent.

## Data Usage
- Data is used for authentication, app functionality, and analytics.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as required by law.
- Data collected from Facebook and Instagram is used solely for OSINT monitoring and analysis purposes.

## Data Storage
- Data is stored securely on our servers and protected by industry-standard security measures.
- Data may be stored temporarily on your device for app functionality.
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

## Third-Party Services
The app uses the following third-party services:
- **Facebook Graph API**: Used to monitor public Facebook pages for cyber patrol purposes.
- **Instagram Graph API**: Used to monitor public Instagram accounts for cyber patrol purposes.
- Data shared with these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

## User Rights
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
- You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information.
- Contact us at cyberpatrol@goapolice.gov.in for privacy-related requests.

## Data Retention
- We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services.
- Monitoring data is retained for law enforcement purposes as required by applicable laws.

## Security
- We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.
- However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy
- The app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to Policy
- We may update this policy from time to time.
- Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
